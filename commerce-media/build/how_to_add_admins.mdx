---
title: 'How to Add Admins'
---

1.  **Log in to Brand Control Center**.
    
2.  Go to **Manage > Communities**.
    
3.  **Edit** the desired community.
    

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/retail_media/build/how_to_add_admins/Communities Listing.jpg" />

  

4.  Go to the **General** section.
    
5.  Scroll to **Admins** and click **+ Add Admin**.
    
6.  Search and add Admins to help manage and enhance your community.
    

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/retail_media/build/how_to_add_admins/Communities-General.jpg" />
