## Download

To download Genuin on your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch you can:

- [Tap here](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/genuin/id1511177838) while on your iOS device
- Search for “Genuin” in the App Store

## Availability

- **Devices** - Genuin is available on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch. While the app is available on iPad, please note that it is not currently optimized.
- **Operating System** - You must be running iOS 15.0 or later to use Genuin. [Learn more](https://support.apple.com/en-in/guide/iphone/iph3e504502/15.0/ios/15.0).
- **Languages** - Currently, we offer the app in English language.

## Keeping Genuin up to date

**To keep the Genuin app updated to the latest version**

1. Open the App Store
2. Tap Today (bottom left)
3. Tap your profile picture (top right)
4. Pull down to refresh the page
5. Tap UPDATE
