## Are you ready to go-to-market?

At a minimum, you can post the white labeled and embed URL to your social media profiles, link in bio, and link trees. You can also make your QR code available at your in-person events, enabling people to simply scan the code and join your community.

Many brands can take their marketing much further by leveraging their existing custom base. Brands that are ready to take their community-led marketing to the next level typically have the following characteristics:

    - Clear Brand Identity: Know your audience and values that matter to them, in which they they can carry forward by Community 
    - Cross-Promotion: Share videos across multiple platforms.
    - Analytics: Use data in the Brand Control Center to optimize content strategy.

## Best Practices

Here are some best practices for brands wanting to build communities and lead their marketing activities with a community-led approach to increase LTV, increase more reach & awareness and most importantly, reduce CAC. 

### WhatsApp

Use WhatsApp communities and convert users to join your own community powered by Genuin. 

    - Create & leverage broadcast lists: Compile a list of customer/user contacts apart of your brand or organization and send a message introducing your communities and how users can join.

    - Use multimedia: Incorporate images, videos, and/or voice messages to make your messages more engaging by showcasing your various communities and how users can join as Community Builders to gain, share, and contribute.

### Reddit

Use reddit communities to drive engagement and convert consumers to join your own community powered by Genuin. 

    - Create a subreddit: Establish a subreddit dedicated to your community. Different ones can be made across communities as well.

        - Utilize stickied posts: Pin important announcements, guidelines on how to be a community member, or even emerging communities for visibility. 

    - Cross-promote: Promote your subreddit across relevant subreddits or social media platforms to attract new community members.

    - Leverage Reddit Ads: Use Reddit's advertising platform to target specific demographics (age, gender, etc.) or interests and promote your subreddit with a direct CTA to your Community.

    - Collaborate with influencers: Partner with influential users a part of the Reddit that are users/moderators within your niche to promote your communities, ultimately reaching a wider audience. 

    - Host AMA (Ask Me Anything) sessions: Arrange AMA sessions with industry experts or notable personalities to increase visibility and attract Community Builders. 

### Discord

Use established communities on your Discord server to drive engagement and convert consumers to join your own community powered by Genuin. 

You can leverage your Discord server by leveraging your channels within your group. Each channel can also, for instance, cater to a specific community of yours. Other effective ways include: 

    - Encourage interaction: Posting in the ‘Announcements’ using the ‘@ mention’ channel alerting all members with a shared link to your community. Regular posts can also be scheduled in this channel to keep members informed, engaged, and incentivized.  

    - Creating awareness: Scheduling a live call with your members that further explain how members of your channel can both gain insight and contribute to your communities as Community Builders.  
    
    - Organize and focus: Create a dedicated server or segment channels for your communities which you can post updates, share new and existing communities and loops. Here, giveaways and contests can also be shared and “pinned”. 

    - Leverage AI: Leverage bots to automate tasks, and manage roles which tie into your communities.

### Email and SMS

Use mass email and SMS outreach programs to increase subscribers for loops and increase your community members count.  

    - Segment your audience: Identify specific segments within your target audience based on demographics, interests, or behavior to tailor your messaging effectively.

    - Rewards for joining your communities to encourage more community members and participation for creating loops.

    - Implement call to actions: Include prominent and actionable calls to action (CTAs) in your emails and SMS messages, guiding recipients to subscribe to loops or join communities.

    - Track & analyze results: Monitor key metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, community sign ups, loops and videos created, etc. to gauge the effectiveness of your outreach efforts and optimize future campaigns.Craft relevant Communities with compelling Loops: Create engaging and relevant content that resonates with your audience's interests and needs, highlighting the benefits of joining your communities.

    - Leverage personalization: Personalize your email and SMS communications with recipients' names, previous interactions, or preferences to enhance relevance and connection with your desired communities & loops.

    - Provide incentives: Offer incentives such as exclusive access, discounts, or rewards.
