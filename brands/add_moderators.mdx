## What is the role of a Moderator?
There can be multiple moderators within each Community. The Moderators can post Loops within the desired Community, but also have access to changing & updating the: display picture, handle, display name, description, categories, social media links, and more.
		
## How can I add Moderators and assign them access?
Select the Community you want to add the Moderator(s) too. Click “Mod tools” and scroll down to “Moderators.” Here, select the + icon where it states “Add Moderators”
