## For Influencers

Genuin is an innovative platform that empowers creators like you to build thriving video-based communities on the open web so you can lead with them. 

We believe partnering with Genuin could be a game-changer for you and your community. We encourage creators such as yourself to utilize and leverage the platform in one of two ways for yourself, and ultimately your audience: 

    1. You can promote selected Communities on Genuin of existing brands you already are familiar with and love! You can then have your audience contribute to these Communities as well for one or various showcased brands which they can start earning rewards for! Additionally, your audience will also have the opportunity to make their OWN Communities for these brands and contribute their content. You can also tell us which specific brand(s) you love, and one of our community specialists can create the brand’s Community for you to publicize to your audience (which they can start contributing to as ‘Community Builders for this brand)

    2. Create your own Community on Genuin (which we can absolutely set up) around an area of interest and/or expertise that relates to you. This can be regarding your merch, digital products, events & workshops, consulting & coaching and more! – The choice is yours! You can then publicize your Community to your audience so they can contribute content via Genuin, leading to attracting brand deals and more for yourself! 

As a Genuin Influencer, you'll have access to powerful tools to:

    - Create exclusive video content for your community
    - Foster authentic connections and engagement
    - Gain valuable audience insights
    - Integrate your community with your existing online presence
    - Unlock brand collaboration and sponsorship opportunities

We're eager to discuss this opportunity with you further and explore how we can tailor our platform to meet your specific needs and goals.
