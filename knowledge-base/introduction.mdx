Welcome to Genuin! 

The Genuin platform empowers brands and their customers, creators and participants to foster vibrant, embedded communities to share knowledge  through bite-sized videos. Videos are seamlessly integrating across your brand’s owned platforms, and the broader web. And it’s all powered by AI.
With Genuin, you can:

- **Build Your Community** - Create “Loops” around topics relevant to the brand

- **Manage Your Influence** - Gain full visibility and transparency to see trends in the community, access participants, and influence conversations

- **Market Your Brand** - distribute the community widely on the open web to directly grow your audiences
