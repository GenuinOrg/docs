## What kind of Community Builder are you?

As a Community Builder, you have the opportunity to tailor an experience for members of your community. Some Community Builders focus their efforts on  sharing knowledge and information while others are more interested in generating enthusiastic discussions. Here are a few scenarios you can consider as you think about how to manage your communities. 

**The Content Curator –** You are passionate about various topics and want to create multiple communities around them. You’ve created communities for cooking, photography, fitness, and travel. By managing multiple communities, you curate specific content for each community, ensuring that members receive relevant and engaging content tailored to their interests.

**The Engager –** You love managing communities for a sports app, including communities for football, basketball, and soccer. As a Community Builder, you engage with members, initiate discussions, and moderate content within each community. Engaging with multiple communities allows you to foster a sense of belonging among members, encourage discussions, and build a vibrant community around each sport.

**The Moderator –** You're managing communities for a parenting app, including communities for new parents, single parents, and parents of teenagers. As a Community Builder, you jump in to moderate discussions, ensure adherence to community guidelines and maintain a positive environment.

**The Collaborator –** You're part of an emerging platform for artists and creatives. As an invited Community Builder, you can manage communities for selected painters, writers, musicians, and photographers. As a Community Builder, you share relevant resources and facilitate collaboration opportunities with other members so they can also contribute to the ecosystem.
