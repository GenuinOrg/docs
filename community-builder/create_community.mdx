## How to create a community?

Communities on Genuin are video-centric. To start building your community, tap the plus (+) icon in the app’s menu bar, and follow the prompts to create your new community.

## How to join an existing community?

When a brand invites you to moderate one of their communities, you’ll receive a notification via email and in the app. Once accepted, you can begin contributing as a moderator within that brand’s community spaces.
