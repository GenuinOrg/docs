## Upload

  

Navigate to **Engage Engine > Import Videos > Uploads**, you can upload the videos to post it.

  
  

To upload the Video you can, drag and drop the videos or upload it from the **Computer**, **Google Drive**, **Dropbox**, and **S3 Bucket**.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdtZbXHqfQaf1ZmdcbiC6sC2ZynVO_I3TDKrOsd-1D7bbwYN9IUhFTDlXczwI7B0v49duOFXpzCSjVpBuTZwI1doIWS931VN_KqKfdnU2g3aY3CMnesw5iUbfoYTmYBHD1yBFHC-CzzaQDiU4_q4qoEZY4z?key=jSkymFA9GGO8XLgzpwiooA)

  

On uploading the video, you can see it is being uploaded and then you can import it to the backend.

  

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeJoCvoobP6-ujwSnUuTxuQQifnuedH1CApMiSRE4AkyzNpsfg6c-Hbocg4spwfO-P2Trg6Nme_G4kG0JgYzF_-veLw0fb0PMLQDF2k0hLE9e8J3mg62p-b7IbBGjWq2M6LgDXF6T78ILmfiwEUd-fCO70?key=jSkymFA9GGO8XLgzpwiooA)

  

Once you clicked the Import button, the videos will be imported to the ecosystem.

  

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeF7lTLe_OO5y4cwt74VaaIb7hHZ5E8K0regrmHeyk2aNOQ3sG28Q0B_OVMPTpRs0flb55AqJ3Olc75TTub18tOJpZr-hz2AZjZDUizJsxO2y09uwtgUpPa3oQSxS6GlUlkqkvYAZZB1A1o9_zDpa-Bmq4?key=jSkymFA9GGO8XLgzpwiooA)

### Approve/Reject Video

  

Based on the content of Video, you can view the suggested Community and Group name. 


Within the Group name your uploaded video will be visible. You can select the video and **Approve** it to post it or you can **Reject** it if you don’t like the video.
