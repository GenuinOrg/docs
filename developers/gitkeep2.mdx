Do you have content in bulk? Upload the short form content to your platform all at once by using our Engage Functionality. 

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 1.png" />


## Upload

  

Navigate to **Engage > Import Videos > Uploads**, you can upload the videos to post it.

  
  

To upload the Video you can, drag and drop the videos or upload it from the **Computer**, **Google Drive**, **Dropbox**, and **S3 Bucket**.

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 2.png" />

  
<Note>

Note: The duration of video shall be less than 300 seconds and up to 2 GB in size. Supported formats to upload files are mp4, mov, avi, wmv, mkv, webm.

</Note>  
  
  
  

**Select Import Destination**: Choose the appropriate Community or Group where you want to import the video.

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 3.png" />

  

Once you have uploaded the video you can apply the below **additional setting** to it. These settings will help you generate engaging content and add it to your community.

## Caption Style

  

-   ### Best Fit
    

This option automatically adjusts captions for optimal readability and placement, ensuring they blend naturally with the video.

-   ### Custom
    

  

Choose from multiple predefined caption styles to match your video's aesthetic. This allows you to select a preferred font, size, and color.

## Data Logs

  

You can see the import status from the **Data Logs > Uploads** and other details like **Name**, **Date**, **Import Status** and **Posted** (Yes/No).

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 8.png" />

  

You can also filter the data based on the **Dates**, **Status**, and **Source**.

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 9.png" />

  

The **Completed** status, depicts that the videos are successfully imported and you can **Approve** it to post on your platform.

  

## Activity

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 10.png" />

  

In the Activity section, you can see how many videos are being **Processed**, **Awaiting Approval**, **Approved**, and **Rejected**.

  

<img height="200" src="/visual-aids/developers/guides/upload/image 11.png" />

  

You can filter the data from **Social Import**, **Uploads**, or **Clipit** or you can directly view the **Pending** to review, **Reviewed**, or **All** Videos.

  
  
  

### Approve/Reject Video

  

Based on the content of Video, you can view the suggested **Community** and **Group** name. Within the Group name your uploaded video will be visible. You can select the video and **Approve** it to post it or you can **Reject** it if you don’t like the video.
